ARIDL GUIDELINES
1. Audible clicks are required
2. Do not use any mods that give you an advantage over other players. the only mod you can use that sort of gives an advantage is Click Between Frames.
3. We do not accept any mods that make click sounds as audible clicks.
4. Use the latest version of the level
5. you may use bug fixed versions of a level
