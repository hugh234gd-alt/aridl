HOW TO SUBMIT A RECORD
1. Go to the ARIDL discord server and find the #submit-records channel.
2. Type in your record with the template: Level Name:[level you beat] Name:[Your GD name] Video Link:[link to a video on YouTube to see the completion (clicks must be audible)] Raw Footage:[if you dont like your voice being heard post a link of 60fps no cut raw footage of the completion from when you start recording to when you finish] Refresh Rate:[your Hz when you beat the level]
3. Wait for a staff member to review your record and approve it.
HOW TO REGISTER AN ACCOUNT
1. First check if you have a record if you do continue to the next step.
2. Go to the ARIDL discord server and find the #player-register channel.
3. Type in your registration with the template: Name:Player name:[A username you want to use.] Record name:[The name you put your record under] Country:[your country] Record level:[The level you put as your record] Hardest:[Hardest level you have beat]
4. Wait for a staff member to review your registration and approve it.
5. Once approved wait for the website to be updated with your ranking.
HOW TO SUGGEST A PLACEMENT OF A LEVEL
1. Go to the ARIDL discord server and find the #opinions channel.
2. Type in your suggestion with the template: Level Name:[name of level you want to make a opinion on] Tier[Tier of level you want to give] Progress:[current progress you have on the level] Verifier or victor:[Specify if you're the verifier or a victor] fluke:[if you fluked the level put yes in here if not pick no] Exact Placement:[the exact placement of the level.] Difficulty:[place the difficulty from the copy area in the tier you want tab in the opinions list:https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/16GZBg_e5kTtLFLufu5J9TXCSCXlCumlYvGrruFm5_lc/edit?usp=sharing] Personal Note:[any personal note you want to add on]
3. Wait for a the list director to add it to the suggestions list.
HOW TO BECOME A STAFF MEMBER
1. Go to the ARIDL discord server and find the #applications channel.
2. Go to the form pinned in the channel and fill it out.
3. Wait for the list director to review your application and approve or disapprove it.
4. If not approved, read why and wait a week and beat 3 insane demons before reapplying.
5. If approved, the list director will put you on trial staff (more about this in the discord server) and you will wait 1-2 weeks depending on your performance.
6. If you do well on trial staff, you will be promoted to the role you applied for and if not you will be contacted with a reason why.
HOW TO SEE UPDATES/ANNOUNCEMENTS TO THE LIST
1. Go to the ARIDL discord server and find the #announcements channel.
2. Go to the channels and roles area and select to get @list ping.
3. Wait for announcements about the list to be posted.
